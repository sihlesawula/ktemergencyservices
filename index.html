INTRODUCTION

KT Emergency Services and Social Support is a leading social care and community NPC Company. We are currently supporting the elderly in Region D. The Organization is currently rendering services to the senior citizens in the region D, in line with city’s social support package. The programme components include Social support, Nutrition, Economic development, Health Lifestyle, Empowerment and Excursion.

OUR VISION

Providing communities with social relieve needs and play a meaningful role in the socio-economic development the vulnerable groups.

MISSION

Our mission is to be committed towards communities in a bigger picture aiming at making a difference in livelihood through awareness of the living conditions such as:

  • Improving their socio-economic standards
  • Access to affordable healthy cost efficient nutrition
  • Chronic management tools through weekly vital records
  • Physical training through different activities

GOALS

Our goal as KT Emergency Services and Social Support are:

  • Direct socio-economic transformation
  • Improving the living standard of communities
  • Health improvement
  • Create deliberate programmes for community longevity

OBJECTIVES

KT Emergency Services and Social Support conducts its social and economic activities within the destitute societies to achieve the following objectives:

  • To improve the socio-economic condition of the society especially the elderly by improving their living situation.
  • Building environment that will keep them healthy
  • Strengthening partnerships that will improve socio-economic conditions in communities especially the elderly people.

Qualified Nurses, Physiotherapists and social workers especially those who are in service training will be sourced from tertiary institutions to assist the elderly who will require their services. They will conduct site visit once a week.

Counselling

We will conduct different types of counselling ranging from mental health to substance abuse through the FAMSA workshops. Youth and former workers will be trained to become counselors to address mental health and substance abuse. Once these counsellors have graduated KT Emergency services and Social support will absorb them and others will be placed to different NGO, NPO and CBO to trade their skills for compensation

Partnership Arrangements

KT Emergency services and Social support is in partnership with Gauteng Education Health and Safety Unit as a service provider where we have 5 Pilot schools 4 being high schools and 1 Primary, Training First aid and basic Fire Level 1 where each schools has the opportunity of 25 beneficiaries who are trained and can operate as first responders in emergency incidents. Skills Training The elderly will be provided with technical and soft skills to keep them active. The training programmes will be highly basic considering their different ages. There will be also activities that they will involve touring places of interest, becoming choir members, social outing (Zoo, Museum, Picnics).

    BACKGROUND INFORMATION

    GOGO on tour program is a program solely focusing on the elderly citizens, were we conduct awareness such as elderly abuse, healthy lifestyle and the importance of taking their chronic medication, and as a result we would like to change the system by encouraging elderly citizens not to default on their medication by offering our service to collect their medication on prescribed dates as well as conducting vital signs and home based care for those who are bed ridden.

PROJECT SCOPE

GOGO ON TOUR is a program standing up for the rights of older and disabled elderly people. It is a movement for change and our goal is to create a fairer community for elder people so they can live safe, Healthy and dignified lives. GOGO ON TOUR is a community network operating under KT Emergency Services and Training as a Non-Profit Organization committed to relieving isolation and loneliness amongst the elderly ..

We believe we are a community in which all older adults are valued and loved. We took note and observed Clinics, Hospitals and Pharmacies are overcrowded with patients who are unstable and are chronic medication patient eg: Diabetes, Hypertension, Epilepsy etc. We would change this. Most of these patients visit the institutions on a monthly basis and this has out tremendous pressure on professional such as nurses, doctors and pharmacist who are already in short supply due to large patients turn over

There is also the economic aspect where in the patient have double expenditure in instance where medicine are not available. We also experienced the risk in being infected and affected by the COVID-19 virus thus making operations in hospitals and clinics unbearable and difficult for health practitioners to work under stress level.
